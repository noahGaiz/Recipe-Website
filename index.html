Recipe for Burger

This is the best tasting burger you will ever have! This burger is very basic
and simple to make all you have to do is follow the steps further down this
recipe.This burger would take around 30 minutes to make.This is one portion
for one person.You are able to remove or add any ingredients you would like
but I don't recommend it because you wont get the maximum amount of tastiness

Ingredients

1 ½ pounds
Fat Ground Beef (80% lean 20%, or ground chuck)
1 tablespoon
Worcestershire Sauce
1 ½ teaspoons
Seasoning Salt
1 teaspoon
Garlic Powder
½ teaspoon
Black Pepper (ground)
4 slices Cheese (optional)
4 Hamburger Buns
Hamburger Toppings (optional: lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, etc)

These are images of ingredients.When looking for your ingredients
they should be very similar to the ones found here. If you cant find
these ingredients near you any type of food will do good.

The best recipe for a burger

1.Add 80% lean ground beef to a large bowl.
2.Add Worcestershire sauce, seasoning salt, garlic powder, and pepper.

3.Mix with your hands until well combined.
4.Divide the beef into fourth quarters.

5.Use your hands to form 4 hamburger patties ¾ inch thick.
6.Use your thumbs to make an indention in the middle of the patties.
7.Grill over medium-high heat for about 4 minutes on each side.

8.Serve on hamburger buns.
9.Top with your favorite traditional burger toppings (lettuce, tomato,
onion, pickles, ketchup, etc.)
